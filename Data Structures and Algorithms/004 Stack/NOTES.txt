What is a stack?

	- In layman's terms, a stack is a collection of things stacked on top of one another. Can be a stack
	  of envelopes, paper, tennis balls, etc.
	  

What is LIFO?

	- Last In First Out. Where we have a stack and we remove / add from the top.
	
	- Push and Pop are the primary actions we perform with a stack.
	
		- When we add something to a stack, the action is called a "push." 
		- When we remove something from a stack, the action is called a "pop."
